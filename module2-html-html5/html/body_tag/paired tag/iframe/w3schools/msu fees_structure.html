MSU fees structure

The fee structure for the Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda (MSU) is as follows:

B.E. / B.Tech:
Tuition fees range from INR 6,000 to INR 84,000

M.E./M.Tech:
Tuition fees range from INR 11,000 to INR 186,000

MBA/PGDM:
Tuition fees range from INR 17,680 to INR 131,960

B.Sc.
Tuition fees range from INR 26,250 to INR 322,380

UG Diploma:
Tuition fees range from INR 1.84 K to INR 1.39 L

B.A. LL.B. (Hons.):
Total tuition fee is INR 210,050

Bachelor of Business Administration:
Total tuition fee is INR 82,290
