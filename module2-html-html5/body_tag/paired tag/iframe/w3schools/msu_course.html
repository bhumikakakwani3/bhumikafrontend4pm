MSU courses


Manonmaniam Sundaranar University (MS University) in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu offers a variety of courses, including bachelor's and master's degrees, as well as certificate courses:

Bachelor's degrees
These include B.Sc. in subjects like computer science, chemistry, and physics, B.A. in subjects like English literature, history, and economics, B.Com. in subjects like accounting and finance, and B.B.A. in business management and administration

Master's degrees
These include M.Sc. in subjects like mathematics, physics, and chemistry, M.A. in subjects like English literature, history, and economics, M.Com. in commerce and accounting, M.B.A. in management, M.C.A. in computer science, and M.Tech. in specialized engineering disciplines

Certificate courses
These include certificates in beauty care and cosmetics, geritics care, home aid care, human care, and a program in Tally

Other courses
These include M.Phil. and Ph.D. programs for advanced research and academic pursuits

MS University also offers an Integrated M.Com program that takes five years to complete.
