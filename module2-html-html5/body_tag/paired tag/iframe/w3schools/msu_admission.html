MSU admission process

the admission process for the maharaja sayajirao university of baroda (MSU baroda) varies by course but genrally involves the following steps:


Apply
Eligible candidates can apply online on the university's official website

Entrance exam
Depending on the course, candidates may need to take an entrance exam, such as the JEE Main or GUJCET

Shortlisting
Candidates are shortlisted based on their performance in the entrance exam and other academic scores

Further rounds
Shortlisted candidates may be called for an extempore test, group discussion, and personal interview

Final selection
Candidates are selected based on their performance in the entrance exam, extempore test, group discussion, and personal interview

Document submission
Shortlisted candidates must submit the required original documents to the college

Admission fee
Selected candidates must pay the MSU Baroda admission fee to complete the admission process
